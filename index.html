--- layout: default title: Let every voice be heard ---

Voxit is an open-source, research-backed, and adaptable platform designed to power collective sense-making. It enables broad listening, ensuring every voice is heard while helping diverse groups move beyond polarization to find uncommon ground - scaling from local communities to entire nation-states.

As a community-governed European digital commons, Voxit is an open ecosystem built to facilitate research and innovation into how algorithms and interfaces shape public debate. By lowering the barrier for experimentation, we ensure that every innovation contributes back to the commons - building a healthier, more transparent foundation for shared understanding and democratic discourse.
